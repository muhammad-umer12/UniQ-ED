unIQ.ED

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. It is a paradisematic country. After the completion of FSC/ A-level, students try to take admission in their desired universities by being prepared for an entry test. In Pakistan, there are numerous centers that provide preparation for the students for ECAT/MCAT/BCAT. They do nothing more than providing past papers and paper patterns and give small lectures. Students have to study hard even after taking admission in centers. Fees of these centers are too high they have different packages and divide entry test preparation into two sections i.e. ECAT for Govt. universities like NED, UET etc. and ECAT for private universities e.g. NUST, FAST.

Our aim is to break that chain which pulls money from the pockets of the parents and provide the bare minimum. In order to break that chain we need a platform where students may get complete guidelines about the criteria of admission of their desired university with course outline, mockup test, practice test, video lectures on time management and stress handling during the entry tests
